## 🧭 What SmartDiscover Does

SmartDiscover helps you find music with plain words. You type a mood, a scene, or a feeling, and it returns Spotify picks that fit what you want. It can also build a playlist with one click.

Use it when you want music for:
- A calm work session
- A late-night drive
- A workout with more energy
- Focus time with fewer vocals
- Songs that match a mood or theme

## ✍️ How to Use SmartDiscover

1. Open the app.

3. Pick the kind of sound you want.

5. Choose the songs you like.
6. Click to create a playlist.

The app reads your prompt, compares it with music data, and ranks songs that fit best.
